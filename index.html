Welcome to the official GST Calculator Hub. Calculate CGST, SGST, and IGST for any transaction in India instantly. This tool provides accurate tax calculations for standard rates (0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, 28%) with options to add (exclusive) or remove (inclusive) GST from the total invoice value.

Mathematical GST Formulas

To add GST to a base amount (exclusive calculation):

GST Amount = Original Cost × (GST Rate / 100)
Total Price = Original Cost + GST Amount

To separate/remove GST from an all-inclusive price (inclusive calculation):

Original Base Cost = Total Price / (1 + (GST Rate / 100))
GST Tax Amount = Total Price - Original Base Cost

CGST, SGST, and IGST Decision Flow

The type of tax applied depends on the place of supply:

  • Intra-state transactions (within the same state): Tax is split 50/50 as Central GST (CGST) and State GST (SGST).
  • Inter-state transactions (across state lines): 100% Integrated GST (IGST) is applied centrally.
